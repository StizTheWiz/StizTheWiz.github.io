Step 1: Download the program here.

Step 2: When the download is finished, click "show in folder."

Step 3: Move the "DesktopGoose" folder out of Downloads and into the Local Disk (C:). The easiest way to do this is to click and drag the file to the local disk button on the left hand side. You could also right click the folder, select copy, and paste into the local disk.

Step 5: Right click the folder, then select "extract all." When the pop-up appears, ignore the text and click "extract." Once the files have been extracted, you can delete the zipped folder if you want to.

Step 6: Open the newly extracted "DesktopGoose" folder, then double click the "GooseDesktop" file. Note that your computer may recognize the program as a virus (it kinda is, but it's not harmful)
