VPN

Okay, so the VPN page is a bit of a work in progress as I've been trying to find something that works ever since Opera stopped working. Currently, all I have is SkyVPN, which is a little buggy but it generally works. Once I find some better options, I will add them here, so be sure to check often.

Step 0: This download requires administrator access. Go the the Home page and click Admin to get it.

Step 1: Download SkyVPN here.

Step 2: When it asks if you want to make changes on your device, click yes. If you're on your school account, you will need to enter ZCM-SystemName\AdminUsername in the username slot and your password.

Step 3: Follow the setup instructions. When it asks if you want it to open on start-up, I suggest choosing No, but it is your choice.

Step 4: You can now browse the internet unrestricted for 500 MB a day (unless you have premium). I know this isn't ideal, so I hope that I will be able to find a VPN with no limits. If you find one, let me know!
