Hollow Knight

Step 1: Download the game here.

Step 2: When the tap opens, click "download."

Step 3: When the download is finished, click "show in folder."

Step 4: Move the "Hollow_Knight" folder out of Downloads and into the Local Disk (C:). The easiest way to do this is to click and drag the file to the local disk button on the left hand side. You could also right click the folder, select copy, and paste into the local disk.

Step 5: Right click the folder, then select "extract all." When the pop-up appears, ignore the text and click "extract." Once the files have been extracted, you can delete the zipped folder if you want to.

Step 6: Double click the newly extracted "Hollow_Knight" folder, then double click the "hollow_knight" file. If something about the Microsoft Store pops up, simply click away from it.

Tips:

  • You can leave this game running in the background if you so choose (although it will use up a decent amount of battery).
  • To close this game, simply click "quit game" in the main menu or close the window.
  • To find the game again, select This PC on your desktop, then select Local Disk (C:). This game will NOT be removed by shutting down your laptop, but will be removed if the laptop is reimaged. Your progress will (probably) be reset, however.
  • This one can be a little laggy at times, so bear with it.
