Plague Inc. Evolved

Step 1: Download the g@me here.

Step 2: When the tab opens, click "download."

Step 3: When the download is finished, click "show in folder."

Step 4: Move the "Plague_Inc_Evolved" folder out of Downloads and into the Local Disk (C:). The easiest way to do this is to click and drag the file to the local disk button on the left hand side. You could also right click the folder, select copy, and paste into the local disk.

Step 5: Right click the folder, then select "extract all." When the pop-up appears, ignore the text and click "extract." Once the files have been extracted, you can delete the zipped folder if you want to.

Step 6: Open the newly extracted "Plague_Inc_Evolved" folder, then scroll all the way down to the bottom and double click the "PlagueIncEvolved" file. Alternatively, you can click the "PlagueIncSC" file if you wish to access the Scenario Creator.

Tips:

  • When booting up this g@me, you may have to press the icon that will appear at the bottom of your screen in order for it to open.
  • When the g@me starts up, a video / trailer thing will play. To skip it, simply click the screen.
  • You can leave this g@me running in the background if you so choose (although it will use up a massive amount of battery).
  • To close this game, click "Exit G@me" on the main menu or close the application.
  • To find the g@me again, select This PC on your desktop, then select Local Disk (C:). This g@me will NOT be removed by shutting down your laptop, but will be removed if the laptop is reimaged. Your progress will (probably) be reset, however.
