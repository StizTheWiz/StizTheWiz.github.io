Bloons TD5

Step 1: Download the g@me here.

Step 2: When the download is finished, click "show in folder."

Step 3: Move the "Bloons_TD5" folder out of Downloads and into the Local Disk (C:). The easiest way to do this is to click and drag the file to the local disk button on the left-hand side. You could also right-click the folder, select copy, and paste it into the local disk.

Step 4: Right-click the folder, then select "extract all." When the pop-up appears, ignore the text and click "extract." Once the files have been extracted, you can delete the zipped folder if you want to.

Step 5: Open the newly extracted "Bloons_TD5" folder, then double-click the file titled "BTD5-Win".

Tips:

  • You can leave this g@me running in the background if you so choose (although it will use up a lot of battery).
  • To close this g@me, just close the window.
  • To find the g@me again, select This PC on your desktop, then select Local Disk (C:). This g@me will NOT be removed by shutting down your laptop, but will be removed if the laptop is reimaged. Your progress will (probably) be reset, however.
